title: "Tax Enforcement and the Informal Economy: Evidence from Administrative Data"

journal: "Journal of Public Economics, Vol. 215, 2023"
coauthors: ["Jane Smith", "Marc Dupont"]
abstract: "We study the effects of targeted tax enforcement on the size and composition of the informal economy using administrative tax records. Exploiting quasi-random variation in audit assignment, we find that enforcement reduces informality by 18% in treated sectors, with significant spillovers to neighbouring firms. Distributional analysis reveals that enforcement gains are concentrated among high-productivity firms."
