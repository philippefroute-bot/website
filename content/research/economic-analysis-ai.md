abstract: "This article examines the impact of artificial intelligence (AI) on economic analysis, revisiting Lucas's critique through the lens of recent technological advances. We highlight the contributions of machine learning-based models and bio-inspired approaches while addressing the current limitations regarding interpretability and applicability. By combining big data and innovative methods, AI overcomes some traditional constraints but requires critical oversight to ensure relevant and ethical economic forecasting."
