I am a Professor of Economics at University Paris-Est Créteil in France. My research focuses on **Law and Economics**, **Public Economics**, and **Public Finance**, with an emphasis on the economic analysis of regulation, taxation, and public institutions.

Before joining University Paris-Est Créteil, I held positions at Banque de France and completed my doctoral studies at University Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne.

My work has been published in leading economic journals and discussed in policy circles. I am also committed to teaching economics at both the undergraduate and graduate levels.

Feel free to reach out by email or consult my CV for more information.
